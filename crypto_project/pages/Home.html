Overview of QR code

  • QR code can be seen as a two-dimensional bar code. QR stands for "quick response".
  • There 40 different versions of QR codes are available today.
  • Users can access the data of QR codes through QR code reader applications.
  • Two-dimensional data store: The square shape QR code can store data in pixels or small black boxes.(vertical and horizontal).
  • error correction feature: If the QR code is very badly scratched or half of it is not available then also it can correct up to the certain limit.
  • Easy to read and access: Nowadays every device with a camera is almost having QR code reader and it takes only a few seconds.
